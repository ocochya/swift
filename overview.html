Swift

Overview of Swift

Swift is a general-purpose programming language developed by Apple Inc. for iOS, macOS, watchOS, tvOS, and Linux.

It first appeared on the 2nd of June, 2014.

Swift is an ideal way to write programs and software for servers, phones, computer desktops, and much more.

Swift is a well respected language, though new, it is rising through the ranks of popularity, and is being used more and more every day!

It's a modern, fast, intuitive, and accessible language that is easily understood by all.

It's easy to use format, and speed, make Swift an excellent choice for everyone, from programming beginners, to experienced programmers.

Swift is also friendly to new programmers! Please feel free to click the links in the navigation bar to discover how to get started, and learn more about this great language!
